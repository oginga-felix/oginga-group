Our Beliefs

Founded on the Word of God and centred on Jesus Christ.

Mori SDA Youth Choir follows the fundamental beliefs of the Seventh-day Adventist Church, which are rooted in the Holy Bible and centred on Jesus Christ.

📖

The Bible

We believe the Bible is the inspired Word of God and the foundation of faith, doctrine, and practice.

✝️

The Trinity

We believe in one God — Father, Son, and Holy Spirit — three distinct but unified persons.

🕍

The Sabbath

We observe the seventh-day Sabbath (Saturday) as a sacred day of worship, rest, and fellowship in obedience to God's commandments.

🌟

The Second Coming of Christ

We believe in the literal, visible, and soon return of Jesus Christ, which is the blessed hope of the church.

💧

Baptism & Salvation

We believe in salvation by grace through faith in Christ, expressed through baptism by immersion.

❤️

Christian Living

We are called to a life of love, service, and stewardship — reflecting Christ in all we do.
